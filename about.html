Who We Are

Kara-Kata is a Nigerian online clothing store based in Lagos State. We sell all types of clothing and attire, from office to casual wear, as well as men's and women's clothing. The store began like any other clothing store, with WhatsApp ad placements, and as the year progressed, we were able to develop a business plan that allowed us to broaden our reach and gain access to a larger customer base. We've been the go-to online store for 5 years, and we're as determined as ever to grow and reach a wider audience. This is only possible through customer relations and process development, which is precisely what we've done.
